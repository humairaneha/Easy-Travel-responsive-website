A Simple Perfect Place To Get Lost

In Bangladesh, the Chittagong Hill Tracts is the region where you can find mountainous terrain. The area is known for its scenic beauty, lush greenery, and diverse indigenous cultures.

  • Keokradong: The highest peak in Bangladesh, located in the Bandarban district, standing at approximately 986 meters (3,235 feet) above sea level.
  • Tajingdong: Also known as "Bijoy," it's the second-highest peak in Bangladesh, located near the Sangu River in Bandarban district.
